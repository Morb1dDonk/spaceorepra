Step 1 — Dispatch
Click Locate & Extract, select an asteroid from the Star Map, and confirm. Your ship launches automatically.
Step 2 — Manifest
When ore arrives, toggle items ON to refine or leave OFF to recycle at 30%. Confirm to proceed.
Step 3 — Processing Profile
Set Speed, Quality, and Yield pips. More quality pips = higher purity grades. Confirm Work Order to start.
Step 4 — Refinery
Watch units process in real time. Inert units are lost. Pristine and Artifact grades carry premium multipliers.
Step 5 — Transfer
Click Prepare Transfer to sell refined ore and bank the credits to your wallet.
Keep fuel topped up — empty tank = stranded. Tow costs 30,000 GC. Buy boost charges from the Shop to cut transit time in half.
